Rules and Regulations

PREAMBLE

  1. The general purpose of these rules and regulations is to direct students behavoiur patterns towards socially acceptable norms thus laying the foundation for good descipline
  2. it is those who keep and abide fully by these rules that will reap to greatest benefit from the academic and social activities.
  3. N.B. breach of each rule attracts punishment(s)

All students must arrive at school by 7:15am and must remain in the school till 5:00pm PCO1 and PCO7

Absentism from school for any period of time without permission is an offence 1st time PCO3 and 2nd time PCO5.

All students must attend and rool calls are made. all subjects are compulsory from S.1 and S.2 students. In S.3 and above shall attend all subjects of their speciality: 1st time PCO1, 2nd time PCO3 and 3rd time PCO5.

Every students must wear school uniform during class hours, official functions and outings.The wearing of fancy untidy clothes and hates, ladies hair style within the school premises are all forbidden PCO4, PCO3,PCO8 for chromic offenders and also uniforms must not be cut short PCO9b

Anti socialnbehaviour such as students being gluttonous, whistling at passerby, howling meaninglessly, hooliganism and misbehaving during school outings and use of vulgar language is prohibited. PCO1, PCO3.

Assualt and fighting including bullying, teasing of new students and disrespectfulness to school authority e.g members of the teaching and non teaching staff is prohibited: 1st time PCO3 and 2nd time PCO3 and for serious injuries PC13

Alcoholism and drunkenness are prohibited while in the school 1st time PC03 and 2nd time PC05.

Smoking and selling of cigarettes are prohibited while in the school 1st time PC03 and 2nd time PC06.

Theft of school property, other students' property and any other person's property, selling and buying of personal effects in the school is prohibited: PC05 and PC11

Every student is expected to respect all forms of authority at all times e.g Head teacher, Deputy Headteacher,teachers, staff prefects and councilors PC12.

Students must show responsibility in the use of school property such as furniture, books laboratory equipment, and building etc. 1st time PC03, 2nd time PC05

Students are not allowed out of bound ie being illegally out of the school coumpound for valid reasons at any time can be granted only by the teacher on duty, deputy headteacher or headteacher. The staffroom, staff quarters and the general office are out of bound to students unless with permission PC02.

Suspended students are not allowed in the school before the end of their suspension periods and no students is allowed to receive or accommodate any such suspeded or expelled student in the school. PC05 and PC06.

Every student must remain in class if a teacher is not present. Every student must remain silent in class in the absence of director of studies, master of the teacher and should be engaged in useful work. PC01.

All visistors must reach the student viaa the office of the head teacher,deputy headteacher on duty. No student will receive visitors or telephone calls during class time unless the matter is considered urgently the authority. PC02.

Students must not allow an outside (person) to come and address them on any issue, be in academic, political or religious matter without the clearance from the headteacherc' office PC12

Unauthorized use of the notice boards is not allowed and no student should tamper with notices. writing on walls, doors, windows, furniture is not allowed PC09.

EVery student must speak english at all times while in school and during all school fuctions. PC01.

All recommended co- curricular activities are compulsory to all students unless medically directed by a doctor. The activities must be done during the right season and time PC12.

Riding a bicycle in the school compound is prohibited. PC01 and consfication of the bicycle.

Strikes and tendency ton incise strikes through behaviour language are illegal PC05 and PC13

Pregnant girls are not allowed in the school. Every test or examination in every subject is compulsory for all students PC01,PC02

Mobile phones, Electrical appliances must not be brought to school and must not be turned/fused during class time. PC08(b)
