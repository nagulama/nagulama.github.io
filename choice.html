Choice of Subjects

The students receive professional guidance from teachers and the careers counsellor in making subject option choices that relate to their careers aspirations. The careers Counsellor manages the process in liason with other teachers. Final options are approved by the DOS.

Number of Subjects Taken

The school adapts to the flexibility of O and A levels but the normal number of subjects recommended by the school in a single sitting is 10 and 3 respectively. For A level science students, the school adapts the flexibility of UNEB i.e 3 principal subjects and two subsidiaries is general paper and either ICT for science students offering mathematics or subsidiary mathematics for those offering Economics
