History of the schools

History of BLLSS

Bukedea lifeline secondary school was opened on 2th Feb 2004 by the Board of Directors who contributed capital for land and its construction. it started with three classes i.e S.1, S.2, and S.3 with a total of about 268 students in the school at kide village bukedea town council. The school is now located here in kide cell Bukedea Town Council.

Bukedea lifeline secondary school is located in kide North cell, Kide ward in Bukedea Town Council. its found along Okunguro Mission road, 200 meters from Mbale Soroti Highway.

The school is a mixed, day and boarding school. its a private school pwned by four members of the board of directors, managed by the Board of Governors, PTA and school administrators. However, the school partners with government in the implementation of universal secondary education (USE) and UPOLET at ordinary and advanced levels respectively.
